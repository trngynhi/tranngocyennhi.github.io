love, to me, is never loud. it is a quiet knowing that you are my safest place, where every thought can land, unpolished, unfinished, yet still welcomed.
sometimes your words wander, arriving a little crooked, as if they took the long route before reaching me.
but beneath them, there is always the same tenderness, the way you stand beside me, never towering, never drifting far. once you said, almost lightly, that you know where you belong.
the way you stand beside me, never towering, never drifting far.
i think you meant a place that answers your heart and somehow that stayed with me, like love is not possession, not distance, not noise,
